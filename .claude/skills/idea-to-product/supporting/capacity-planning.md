# Capacity Planning Template

**Purpose:** Plan for hiring, team structure, and operational capacity when your product involves manual/human components.

---

## When to Use

Use this template when your product includes:
- Manual fulfillment (done-for-you services)
- Content creation by humans
- Customer support requirements
- Operations that don't scale automatically
