# Competitor Brief Template

**Purpose:** Deep analysis of individual competitors. Create one brief per competitor (2-5 total).

---

## Research Prompt (Copy to Claude Desktop)

```
Research [COMPETITOR NAME] comprehensively and produce a Competitor Brief using this structure. Use web search to gather:

1. Product features and pricing
2. Target market and positioning
3. Company background (funding, team size, history)
4. User reviews and sentiment (G2, Capterra, Reddit, Twitter)
5. Technical approach (if discoverable)
6. Content and marketing strategy
7. Strengths and weaknesses

Be thorough but objective. I need accurate information to make strategic decisions.

Output the brief in the template format below.
```
